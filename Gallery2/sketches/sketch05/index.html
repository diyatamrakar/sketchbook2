Sketch #5: Commuting Iteration with Else

What is the new 'else' behavior? In this iteration, I added an else statement that triggers a "resting" mode. When the mouse is not pressed, the circle returns home, changes color to pink, and pulses in size.

How does this change the experience? By adding a clear alternative behavior, the "Home" state feels like an intentional destination rather than just a lack of input. It creates a cycle between the energy of commuting and the calm of being at home.
