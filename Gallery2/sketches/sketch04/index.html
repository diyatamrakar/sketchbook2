Sketch #4: Commuting

What verb are you encoding? I am encoding the verb "Commuting."

What rule governs the behavior? The system follows a "Home/Work" rule: the circle stays at a fixed 'Home' position (the grey circle) unless the mouse is pressed. When the user clicks, the circle 'commutes' to the mouse location.

What changes over time? The circle's state changes from 'resting' to 'active' based on user input. Additionally, the constant jitter makes the circle feel like it is always in motion, representing the restless energy of travel.
