Sketch 01: Warm

For this sketch, I chose the adjective Warm to create a sense of glowing heat and comfort. I used direct manipulation with the mouse to move the light source, combined with an accumulation pattern that leaves fading ripples behind. The size of the light pulses over time using a sine wave, and I programmed the ripples to "snapshot" that pulse size at the moment they are created. I adjusted the decay rate of the ripples to be very slow (0.5 alpha per frame), allowing a soft trail of light to linger on the deep red background without cluttering it.
