Sketch 02: Cozy

The adjective Cozy inspired this sketch, focusing on the feeling of being nestled within a safe boundary. I used a constraint pattern where the movement of the square is limited to the inside of a drawn box, regardless of where the mouse moves on the canvas. As the square moves toward the edges of its boundary, its size and scale shift slightly to suggest it is settling into a corner. I adjusted the constraint math several times to ensure the square never "breaks" out of its frame, maintaining that sense of security.
